What is STEM?

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. These fields are essential for solving real-world problems and creating the future.

S: Science

Science is all about curiosity—asking questions, conducting experiments, and discovering how the world works, from the tiniest atoms to the vastness of space.

Science Buddies – Explore thousands of science experiments and projects

T: Technology

Technology is the use of tools, machines, and computers to solve problems and make life easier. It's about innovation, coding, and creating the gadgets of tomorrow.

Code.org – Learn to code and create your own apps and games

E: Engineering

Engineering is the art of building—designing bridges, inventing machines, and solving real-world challenges with creativity and logic.

TryEngineering – Discover engineering activities, games, and career info

M: Mathematics

Mathematics is the language of patterns and numbers. It helps us solve puzzles, measure, predict, and understand the universe's hidden rules.

NRICH Maths – Dive into fun math puzzles, games, and challenges
